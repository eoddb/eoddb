Tier List Weight

Tier List is weighted heavily for early game lack of resources, small/no 5 star Eidos pool for new players and heavily prioritizes units that are versatile and can fit into a large array of rosters rather than other tier lists. If you have every unit in the game, the Top picks will still be relevant, but the tiers below may be more subjective as you can push pure aspect teams with enough resources.

EOD Top Picks

CamcorgiBlaster

All round top pick. Solid damage, crowd control and flexibly plays as solo memory or into Memory (3) teams. Herocrates memory and companion synergy plus 4 star Shilian memory buffs. Available for free completing Apex Club quests.

StarspineMastermind

4 star unit with great magic damage, Armour/Magic Def shred and stun for crowd control. Light aspect synergy is easy to proc, only needing 2 and has a wide 2nd choice available. Rarely played solo light.

CinechelProtector

Top tier tank that is a physical specialist. Shreds enemy physical armour from the frontline and has great self sustain regen — healing based on damage taken plus a flat amount while skill is active. Camcorgi + Cinechel flexibly plays into most rosters without detriment, last slot for Memory (3) either a dps or tank. Available to catch for free in the overworld in CBT.

ApollinisProtector

Best 4 star unit. Top pick to deserve a nerf for official launch, heals based off max health (28%) and gaining more healing the more health lost. Also heals one ally with the lowest HP. Light (2) often super easy to proc with another 4 star — Starspine. Upside potential if there is a great Light Eidos.

DragamourMascot

Heals, damages, shields and is relatively bulky for a mascot. Scales excellently with Rapport (additional copies) and is the best flex solo pick. Usually fits into almost all team comps except full Hate. Little alternatives from 4/3 star roster makes me recommend this as the best 5 star to pick up if they make it limited — was able to get for free from guidebook in CBT.

HerocratesEidos · Memory

Cost efficiency, flexibility and versatility makes us rank Herocrates as our top pick. Companion is one of the best units in the game and he buffs its damage as well as giving memory units more damage. Skill funnels large amounts of energy to a single unit and procs Echo. Pairs well with 4 star Shilian to offer better Memory strength while leaving roster well open for future units and building specialist teams (Hate/Dark).

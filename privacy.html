Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

EODdb.com ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. There are no accounts, sign-ups, or forms on this site. We use third-party services that may collect anonymous usage data:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

This data is anonymous and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

EODdb.com uses cookies only through Google Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or personalization. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external services including Discord and Ko-fi. These services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense in the future. If implemented, this policy will be updated to reflect any additional data collection related to ad personalization.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties beyond the analytics services described above.

Opt Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings
  • Using your browser's "Do Not Track" feature

Children's Privacy

EODdb.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

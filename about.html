About EODdb.com

EODdb.com is a free, community-driven multi-game database built by EODGamer. The name stands for "End Of Day" — a place to wind down, look up game data, and plan your next session.

What We Do

We provide detailed game databases, guides, and statistical analysis to help players make informed decisions. Every data point is sourced from in-game information and organized for quick reference — no filler, no fluff.

Games Covered

Honkai: Nexus Anima — complete Nexus Anima database with aspect synergy breakdowns, rarity analysis, and team-building reference.

Path of Exile 2 — builds, item databases, and guides. Coming soon.

More games will be added over time based on community interest.

Commentary

Graph shows currencies plotted with respect to Divine Orb. A currency moving up means it is inflating against Divine Orbs. The Y-axis is normalized to a particular reference date within each league — i.e. 10 on the Y-axis means the currency has increased 10 times in value relative to the reference day's Divine Orb value.

Date range is 45 days after the reference date is established per league for analysis. Negative dates represent possibly volatile data which we exclude by referencing.
